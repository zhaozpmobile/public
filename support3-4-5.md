# 3-4-5

**An ancient Chinese strategy game, reborn for your phone.**

3-4-5 (inspired by 三斜小井) is a free, offline-first board game available on iOS and Android. Play against a tunable AI or pass the device to a friend for local two-player matches.

## Frequently asked questions

### How do I play?
The game includes a built-in interactive **Rules** tutorial. Open the app, tap the rules icon from the main menu, and follow the walkthrough. In short: form a line of 3, 4, or 5 of your pieces in a row to capture opponent pieces. Reduce your opponent below the minimum required to play and you win.

### Is the app free? Are there in-app purchases?
The app is free. It is supported by a single non-intrusive banner ad at the bottom of the screen. There are **no** in-app purchases, no forced video ads, and no paywalls.

### Does the app work offline?
Yes — fully offline. The only network use is for loading the banner ad. No account, login, or signup is required.

### Does the app collect my data?
3-4-5 itself does not collect personal data. The banner ad is served by Google AdMob, which uses an advertising identifier to deliver ads. See the [Privacy Policy](privacy.md) for full details.

### How do I adjust AI difficulty?
Open **Settings** from the main menu and choose your preferred AI level. The AI scales from a relaxed companion to a punishing opponent.

### Can I play with a friend on the same device?
Yes — choose **Local Multiplayer** mode and pass the device back and forth between turns.

### How do I reset my score history?
**Settings → Clear History**. This deletes all locally stored game records.

### Will there be online multiplayer?
Not currently planned. The game is designed as an offline-first, local-first experience. If enough players request it, it may be added in a future update.
